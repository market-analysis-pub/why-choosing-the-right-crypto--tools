## Key Criteria for German Traders

### Regulatory Compliance
- **BaFin Licensing**: Required for deposit/withdrawal of EUR via SEPA in Germany
- **MiFID II Compliance**: Portfolio protection, segregated client funds
- **AML/KYC**: Full identity verification required

### Payment Methods
- **SEPA Transfers**: Standard for German traders (€0–€5 fee, 1–3 days)
- **Bank Card**: Higher fees, faster deposits
- **PayPal**: Limited availability; check exchange policies

### Fee Structure
- Compare maker/taker spread
- Withdrawal fees (typically €2–€10 EUR)
- Deposit fees vary by method
- Volume-based discounts available

### Security Features
- 2FA: TOTP + hardware key recommended
- Cold storage: 70–95% of customer assets
- Insurance/compensation fund
- Regular security audits

## Compliance Notes

**Germany-Specific Regulations (2026)**:
- All exchanges must hold MaRKo (Market Abuse Regulation) compliance
- Staking rewards are taxed as income
- Capital gains taxed after 10 years hodling (exemption rules)
- BaFin regulates custody and spot trading; derivatives subject to additional oversight
