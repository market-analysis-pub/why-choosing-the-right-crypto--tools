- [ ] Review withdrawal limits and settlement times (FAST typically 1-3 hours)

## Tax Implications Summary

- **Capital Gains**: Not subject to capital gains tax in Singapore; gains on Bitcoin disposal are not taxable.
- **Income from Mining/Staking**: If self-employed or mining as business, profits taxable as business income.
- **Holding Period**: No holding period to qualify for tax exemption; all long-term and short-term gains exempt.
- **Corporate Holdings**: If held by company, gains exempt under Individual Relief; staking rewards taxable.
- **Record Keeping**: Maintain transaction receipts and timestamps for audit purposes (IRAS typically accepts 5-year lookback).
