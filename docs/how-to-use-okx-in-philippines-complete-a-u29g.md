# OKX Philippines Trading Checklist

A curated guide for Filipino crypto traders accessing OKX, covering compliance, payment methods, and account setup.

## KYC & Account Setup
- [ ] Email verification
- [ ] Basic identity verification (1-10 USD daily limit)
- [ ] Intermediate verification with ID + address (100-500 USD daily limit)
- [ ] Advanced verification for higher limits

## Payment Methods (P2P Trading)
- **GCash** (primary Filipino method)
- **Bank transfers** (BDO, BPI, Metrobank)
- **COD (Cash on Delivery)** in select areas

## Withdrawal Options
- GCash transfers (fastest for small amounts)
- Direct bank withdrawals (24-48 hour settlement)
- P2P seller negotiation for alternative methods

## Compliance Notes
- OKX operates under Philippines monetary authority guidelines
- SEC classification: not classified as securities (varies by token)
- Report crypto gains on annual tax filing (BIR Form 1605)
- KYC mandatory after first $1,000 transaction

## Trading Tips for Filipinos
- Start with P2P trading to understand market spreads
- GCash has lower fees than bank transfers for <₱10,000
- Verify seller reputation (5+ transactions, >95% rating)
- Keep records for tax compliance
