## BNM Framework

- **Authority**: Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) regulates digital assets
- **Current Stance**: Unlicensed exchanges (OKX, Binance) operate in gray zone
- **Key Risk**: No investor protection; accounts subject to freeze/regulatory action

## Tax Obligations

### Income Tax
- Crypto trading gains classified as business income (if frequent/professional) or capital gains (if occasional)
- Reporting required to Malaysian Inland Revenue Board (LHDN)
- Tax rate: 0–30% depending on residency and income classification

### Record-Keeping
- **Maintain**: Trade history, buy/sell prices, dates, transaction fees
- **Duration**: 5+ years (LHDN audit requirements)
- **Recommendation**: Use portfolio tracking tools with export capability

### Reporting Timeline
- File annual tax return (Form B) if income exceeds threshold
- Deadline: Typically May 31 following tax year
